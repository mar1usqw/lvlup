Our Experience

Level Up Construct Ltd has over 19 years of experience in the construction industry, specialising in residential and commercial refurbishment and fit-out projects across London and surrounding areas.

We manage projects from initial planning through to final handover, ensuring clear communication, compliance with building regulations, and high standards of workmanship at every stage.

Warranty & Quality Assurance

All our projects are backed by a clear contractor warranty, providing confidence and peace of mind for our clients.

  • Residential building warranty covering structural elements
  • A simple and efficient claims process, not reliant on proof of negligence
  • Project-specific warranty coverage for each build
  • A transparent and straightforward underwriting process

A typical contractor warranty confirms that no defects or deficiencies will develop in the construction work for a defined period, usually 12 months. During this time, we commit to returning to site to repair or replace any defective work promptly and professionally.
